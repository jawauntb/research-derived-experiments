## 3. The lab's discipline (do not violate — it's the whole point)

- **Pre-register gates BEFORE running** (commit a `preregistration.md`), with a kill
  criterion and the strongest "old-regime shortcut" that should almost work.
- **Transfer/held-out is the headline, never i.i.d. saturation.** A wall of `1.000`
  i.i.d. gates is a red flag (exhausted attractor), not a success.
- **Report honest negatives and partials.** 2/3 seeds is "2/3", not "passes". Leave
  `all_pass=False` rather than redefining gates post hoc.
- **Do NOT over-fit metrics/rubrics to answers you already know** — that is the same
  gaming as the apophenia the discovery-EWS demonstrated.
- **Breakthrough vs. nonsense discriminator**: a destabilization counts as productive
  ONLY if the new regime (a) survives transfer/OOD and (b) bears external load — and
  that verdict is *extrinsic and lagging*. Rising surprisal alone proves nothing
  (it's identical for breakthrough, delusion, and losing the plot).
