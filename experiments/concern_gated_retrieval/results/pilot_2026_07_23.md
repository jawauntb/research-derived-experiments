## Decision

The implementation-level composition passes its three noncompensatory gates on
192 deterministic episodes (64 seeds x base, sparse, and noisy regimes).
Rarity-corrected two-sided PageRank selects the registered load-bearing node
at hit@1 `1.000`, compared with `0.0052` for the better one-sided policy. The
goal-conditioned reservoir/ridge filter has precision `1.000` and recall
`1.000` within top-three nominations. Maximum PageRank fixed-point residual is
`7.83e-13`.

This supports only:

> On this role-authored graph family, the implemented dual-source diffusion and
> bounded-observer utilization check discriminate the registered synthetic
> roles under the frozen regimes and seeds.

It does not support claims about human memory, semantic meaning, selfhood,
general intelligence, or deployment.

## Epiplexity controls

The load-bearing structured future scores `2.4091` bits in every registered
episode. Seed-shuffled alarm controls range from `0.1740` to `0.5965` bits,
seed-shuffled coincidence traps range from `0.2035` to `0.6880` bits, and all
constant controls score `0.0000` bits. The worst registered margin is `1.7210`
bits, so the `0.75`-bit threshold accepts only the load-bearing future in these
fixtures.

This is an implementation sanity check, not an independent semantic result:
the simulator authors which future is structured and goal-conditioned.

## Online-care residual

The online update raises commitment and family anchor weights from `1.0` to
`2.0`, while the alarm anchor remains at `1.0`. But coincidence hit@1 is
already `1.000` before learning, every selected training probe is
load-bearing, and no negative alarm update is exercised. The registered
exploratory gate passes mathematically but is non-discriminating. No claim that
the care manifold was learned, self-defined, or recovered from misspecification
is promoted.

## Preserved alternatives and next bottleneck

The receipt retains context-only, care-only, additive, unverified coincidence,
constant-future, and noise-future results. The decisive next experiment is a
new preregistration with learned or withheld graph edges, an adversarially
misspecified initial care prior, and task utility sealed from the retrieval
policy. That design must make the additive/product and learned/oracle
comparisons non-ceiling before any stronger mechanism claim.
