## Question

When do a decodable feature, concern-shaped category, or self/world split earn
the stronger descriptions *causally used*, *content-bearing*, *real pattern*,
or *self-model*?

## Perspective scan

- **Practitioner:** The useful common denominator is a testable chain: feature
  acquisition history -> consumer/mechanism -> intervention dose-response ->
  held-out counterfactual transport. A probe or single patch is only one link.
- **Academic:** The sources disagree about what grounds representation and
  reality—etiological function, causal structuring, or predictive/compressive
  leverage—so the papers must not present them as synonyms.
- **Skeptic:** All four vocabularies can be honorific labels on experimenter-
  supplied objectives. Wrong-role, yoked-value, rival-pattern, and shifted-world
  controls are therefore mandatory.
- **Incentives:** Mentalistic language makes a result memorable and publishable,
  which creates pressure to upgrade C1 supplied weights into C2 system-relative
  concern or C3 phenomenal mattering.
- **Historian:** These debates predate modern interpretability. The program’s
  novelty is an executable comparison among their operational consequences, not
  rediscovery of their distinctions.

## Contradiction map

1. **Etiological function versus present causal use.** Millikan can withhold
   content from a newly trained but causally effective feature whose relevant
   history/function is absent; Dretskean and intervention-based analyses may
   still call it behaviorally structuring. Record both judgments.
2. **Single real decomposition versus pattern pluralism.** A null action may
   identify one decomposition under an SCM, while Dennett-style rival patterns
   remain equally real if they compress and predict as well. Do not call the
   chosen gauge metaphysically unique without a separating counterfactual.
3. **Causal-role category versus natural kind.** A category can guide action
   without Boyd’s homeostatic/projectible mechanism. “Useful object” is a lower
   claim tier than “natural kind.”

All lenses agree that decodability is insufficient. None of these sources makes
activation patching automatically valid off-manifold, so abstraction and support
checks remain an independent methodological requirement.

## Synthesis and next gates

Current claim boundary: the program can claim graded causal dependence relative
to declared interventions and commitment surfaces, supervised causal-role
categories in bounded tasks, and model-relative self/world identification under
a stated SCM. It must withhold teleosemantic content, natural-kind realism,
metaphysically unique selfhood, and phenomenal interpretation until their extra
conditions are tested.

Smallest decisive experiments:

1. **Dretske/use gate:** compare known-causal, equally decodable-only, and null
   features with mass-normalized dose-response, wrong-subspace controls, and
   transfer across two commitment surfaces.
2. **Millikan/Boyd object gate:** shift causal roles after training and compare
   supervised-label, consumer-grounded, wrong-role, and yoked-role categories.
   Measure misrepresentation, cross-modal projectibility, and mechanism
   dependence.
3. **Dennett/self gate:** compare null, temporal, source-label, and rollout gauge
   fixers on held-out counterfactual prediction and description length. A unique
   self claim requires a preregistered separating intervention; a tie yields
   gauge pluralism.

Peer-review confidence: 8/10 for the claim boundaries, 6/10 for the proposed
operational translations. The weakest link is mapping etiological “proper
function” onto trained artificial systems; publish that mapping as an analogy
unless a selection/history criterion is explicitly defended.
